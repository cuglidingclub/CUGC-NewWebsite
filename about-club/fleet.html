CGC Gliders

For training we are using the two-seaters of the Cambridge Gliding Centre (CGC)and CUGC members also have access to the other aircraft that CGC provides. Forinformation about their fleet visit their website http://www.camgliding.uk/about/facilities/

CU

We also own our own high-performance glider CU, an Alexander Schleicher ASW 19b,a single seater, which members can use at very low rates (currently £18 perhour).
