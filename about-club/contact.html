Contact

How to contact us

Probably the fastest way to contact us, is via ourFacebook page. If you do not have Facebook or prefer not to use it, you can reach us via the following e-mail addresses:

  • enquiries@cugc.org.uk for general enquiries

Mailing lists

We use mailing lists to organise several different parts of our activities:

  • soc-gliding-trialflights@lists.cam.ac.uk (Mailing list for trial flights)
  • soc-gliding@lists.cam.ac.uk (Main mailing list, members only)
  • soc-gliding-transport@lists.cam.ac.uk (Transport mailing list, members only)
  • soc-gliding-asw19@lists.cam.ac.uk (ASW 19b information, members and alumni only)
  • soc-gliding-trialflights@lists.cam.ac.uk (Mailing list for trial flights)

There is also a mailing list by CGC for general club matters, to which members can (and should) subscribe: http://traka.me.uk/mailman/listinfo/cgc-news_traka.me.uk Please do not try to subscribe to a mailing list if you are not eligible to do so.
