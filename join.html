Join CUGC

Members get a wide range of benefits including:

  • Cheaper flights
  • Regular lifts to the airfield
  • Personalised instruction
  • Expeditions to other gliding clubs
  • Social events

Student Membership

Annual club membership is not to be confused with trial flights.

Eligibility
  • You are a student at Cambridge or Anglia Ruskin University.
  • You meet the fitness criteria for trial flights.

By becoming a member of CUGC you agree to our Constitution, ourCode of Conduct and the Terms and Conditions. Membershipcosts £140 (£160 without a trial flight), and lasts one year or until you graduate, whichever isearlier.

Staff and Alumni Membership

Eligibility
  • You are a current member of staff or alumnus/alumna of Cambridge University.

You canjoin the Cambridge Gliding Centre (CGC) as an ordinary memberand then participate in CUGC activities. Make sure to subscribe to ourmailing lists.
